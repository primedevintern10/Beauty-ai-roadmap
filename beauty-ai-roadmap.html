Sales Tool
A digital salesperson — personalised, intelligent, and built for Sri Lankan shoppers
What is this system?
The sales system functions as a digital salesperson, focusing on the promotion and sale of products. Its key feature is the ability to offer personalised product recommendations, specifically addressing customer concerns such as skincare requirements, product types, and allergies.
Two primary audiences
B2C
Direct consumers
Individual users visiting the beautymart.lk platform. They seek tailored product suggestions based on their specific needs — skincare goals, product types, or allergy considerations.
B2B
Enterprise users & partnerships
Organisations and companies interested in licensing or integrating the sales system module into their own platforms to boost sales and increase profitability.

    Platform
      PWA — what does this mean?
      Progressive Web App — a website that behaves like a phone app
      Instead of asking users to download anything from an app store, they simply visit the website and tap "Add to home screen." It then sits on their phone like a normal app — with its own icon, works offline, and loads fast. No app store needed, no download required.
      Works on any phone No app store download Sits on home screen like an app Works offline too Cheaper to build than a native app
      Our approach
      Best offer logic (default) + sponsored ranking (paid)
      The chatbot recommends the product based on the user's skin profile, then resolves who to buy it from based on price + delivery + sponsored ranking. This keeps the recommendation trustworthy — it's still picking the right product for your skin — while giving us a revenue stream from vendor placement fees.

      What is a widget?
      🔌
      A widget is a small piece of software that sits inside another website
      Think of it like a pop-up chat bubble you see on the bottom-right corner of many websites — like a bank, a shop, or a delivery service. That bubble is a widget. It is not part of the website itself; it was added by pasting a single line of code. Our chatbot works exactly the same way. Any vendor can paste our script tag into their website and the full sales chatbot appears — no technical team, no rebuild, no app to install.
      One line of code to install Works on any website No rebuild needed Like Intercom or Tidio

      Independence
        How a vendor gets the chatbot on their website
        1
        Vendor registration
        The vendor registers on our platform to list their products for sale.
        2
        Product instance creation
        Once registered, the vendor's products are saved in our database — creating a dedicated data instance for them.
        3
        The vendor has their own website
        The vendor operates their own external products website on their own domain.
        4
        The vendor wants our chatbot
        The vendor wishes to implement our sales chatbot on their personal website.
        5
        Widget embedding
        The vendor copies and pastes a single script tag into their website — that's all the technical setup needed.
        6
        Identity verification
        The vendor uses the widget interface to verify their identity and prove ownership of their account.
        7
        Database connection
        Upon successful verification, the widget connects to the vendor's specific product instance in our database.
        8
        Chatbot is live
        The sales chatbot is now fully operational on the vendor's webpage, using only their own listed products.
